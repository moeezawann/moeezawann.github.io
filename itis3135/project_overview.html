FMJ Shooting Range

Project Overview

This project is desgined for a friend who loves going to the shooting range on the weekends. He one day hopes to open up his own shooting range and wants to see what a potential website could look like! The purpose of this website is to help customers or people interested in going to the range. The website will provide answers about the range, as well as help customers in their shopping experience.
