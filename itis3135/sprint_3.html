Project Journey

What I learned:

It takes a team not a person, this project was a good example for how teamwork is needed to get a working solution. It required everyone to be hands on and continusouly be practive on where improvemnts and enhancements are needed. Without the entire team working all hands on deck the completion of this appplication would not have been possible.

Challenges:

Time constraints is one of the big ones, knowing everyone can only dedicate a certain amount of time per week. So working wihtin these constraints was important, secondly for me connecting our application to our back end was also a but difficult but once the connection was made things setelled down.

Skills gained:

I got to gain experince in both back end as well as front end development. I got the chance to expirement with technolgies I had not worked with before, as well as honing my own technical skills. Alongside of this building connections with my fellow classmates teaching and leanirng from one another were also important skills gained and taught!
