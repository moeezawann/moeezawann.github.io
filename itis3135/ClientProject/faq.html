Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open from 9 AM to 9 PM, Monday through Saturday.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

No, we provide all necessary equipment for rent at our facility.

Are there any age restrictions?

Yes, participants must be at least 18 years old to use the range.

Do you offer training sessions?

Yes, we offer training sessions for beginners and advanced shooters.
