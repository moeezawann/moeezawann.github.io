Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours? ▶

We are open from 9 AM to 9 PM, Monday through Saturday. Depending on weather.

Do I need to bring my own equipment? ▶

No, we provide all necessary equipment for rent at our facility, but you are more than welcome to bring your own.

Are there any age restrictions? ▶

Yes, participants must be at least 18 years old or have a parent/legal guardian with them.

Do you offer training sessions? ▶

Yes, we offer training sessions for beginners and advanced shooters.

Is there something I cannot bring? ▶

No, since we are an outdoor range you can bring whatever you like. Please make sure weapons are unloaded until you are inside the range.
